Project Team 08: Visualizing Police Incidents in Boston, DS4200 F20

Aidan Doherty, George Barker, Jeffery Owen

Service-Learning Course Project as part of DS 4200 F20: Information Visualization, taught by Prof. Cody Dunne, Data Visualization @ Khoury, Northeastern University.

Abstract

Our goal is to build an interactive visual of all Boston police inci-dents, field searches and field interrogations over the past five years.Currently, all of these reports are available as public records to any-one who wishes to view them, but they are obscured by nature asthey are simply listed in a massive spreadsheet. A user could notmake any conclusions about how BPD acts in the field by scanningthese spreadsheets, but an interactive visual would allow citizens toview how BPD behaves and demand change if they see fit.
